# About

Acme Widgets Inc. manufactures and sells industrial-grade widgets for the manufacturing sector. We've been in business since 2012, serving over 200 clients across North America and Europe.

Our focus is quality and fast turnaround. Every widget is tested before shipping.

# Services

- Standard Widgets — $10/unit, minimum order quantity 50 units, ships within 5 business days
- Premium Widgets — $25/unit, minimum order quantity 10 units, ships within 3 business days
- Custom Widgets — quote-based, minimum order quantity 100 units, 2-week lead time
- Bulk Contracts — annual pricing for orders exceeding 10,000 units/year, contact sales for terms

# FAQs

## What is your minimum order quantity?

50 units for Standard Widgets, 10 units for Premium Widgets, and 100 units for Custom Widgets. Bulk contracts start at 10,000 units/year.

## Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to North America and Europe. International shipping is calculated at checkout. Orders outside these regions should contact sales@acmewidgets.com for a quote.

## What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for defective products. Custom Widgets are non-returnable unless they fail to meet the agreed specification. Contact support@acmewidgets.com to initiate a return.

## Can I get a sample before placing a large order?

Yes. We offer sample packs of 5 units (Standard or Premium) at regular per-unit pricing. Contact sales@acmewidgets.com to request a sample.

## What materials are your widgets made of?

Standard Widgets use industrial-grade ABS plastic. Premium Widgets use reinforced nylon composite. Custom Widgets can be manufactured in a range of materials depending on the specification.

## Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Orders over 500 units receive a 10% discount. Orders over 5,000 units receive 20%. Bulk contract pricing is negotiated separately.

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfer, credit card, and NET-30 terms for established accounts. New customers pay upfront for the first order.

## How do I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number by email once your order ships. For questions about an existing order, email support@acmewidgets.com with your order number.
